group-students.com
Group your students quickly!

View project on GitHub ↗

Here you can quickly and easily assign students to the desired number of groups.

The grouping is done:
- randomly
- by preference for collaboration
- against the preference for collaboration
- by heterogeneity of performance levels (1-3)
- by homogeneity of performance levels (1-3)

The table must include the names of the students to be assigned in its first column.

Optionally, the following columns may contain the students’ preferred partners for collaboration and/or their performance level (1-3).
